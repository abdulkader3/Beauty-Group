DO YOU KNOW HOW HAIR SPRAY WORKS?

January 20, 2020

Hair spray is one of the most commonly used styling products. But very few people know how it actually works.

✨ How It Holds Hair?

Hair spray contains holding agents that create a thin layer around the hair, keeping it in place.

✨ Types of Hair Sprays

There are light, medium and strong hold sprays depending on styling needs.
