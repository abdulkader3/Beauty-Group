What is Spa Facial & Hair Cut?

Our combined Spa Facial & Hair Cut service offers the ultimate beauty transformation. Enjoy a professional spa facial treatment that rejuvenates your skin, followed by an expert hair cut that enhances your natural features and style. It's the perfect way to refresh your entire appearance.

Key Benefits

Radiant Skin: Professional facial treatment gives you a glowing complexion

Expert Styling: Professional hair cut tailored to your face shape

Relaxation: Dual pampering experience in one visit

Total Transformation: Complete beauty refresh

Time Efficient: Get two services in one appointment

Treatment Details

  • Frequency: Monthly for facials, every 4-6 weeks for hair
  • Suitable for: All hair and skin types
  • Results: Glowing skin and polished appearance
