What is Papaya Sesame Body Wrap?

Our signature Papaya Sesame Body Wrap is a luxurious full-body treatment that combines the natural enzymes of ripe papaya with the nourishing properties of sesame oil. This tropical-inspired treatment exfoliates, moisturizes, and revitalizes your entire body, leaving your skin incredibly soft and glowing.

Key Benefits

Natural Exfoliation: Papaya enzymes gently remove dead skin cells

Deep Hydration: Sesame oil penetrates and moisturizes skin deeply

Brightening: Reveals luminous, glowing skin

Antioxidant Protection: Rich in vitamins and natural antioxidants

Aromatherapy Benefits: Relaxing tropical scents enhance the experience

Treatment Details

  • Duration: 75 minutes
  • Frequency: Monthly for best results
  • Suitable for: All skin types, especially dry skin
  • Results: Immediate softness and glow, lasting hydration

Treatment Process

Consultation on skin type and preferences

Gentle body exfoliation with papaya paste

Complete body wrap with warming herbal compress

Luxurious sesame oil massage

Finishing with moisturizing cream
