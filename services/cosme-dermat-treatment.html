What is Cosme Dermat Treatment?

Our Cosme Dermat Treatment is a professional skin rejuvenation service that combines advanced dermatological techniques with premium cosmetic products. This treatment is designed to address various skin concerns including acne, wrinkles, dark spots, and uneven skin tone.

Key Benefits

Deep Cleansing: Professional extraction and cleansing to remove impurities and dead skin cells

Skin Renewal: Stimulates collagen production for youthful, plump skin

Brightening Effect: Reduces dark spots and evens out skin tone

Anti-Aging: Minimizes fine lines and wrinkles for a more youthful appearance

Treatment Details

  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Frequency: Recommended monthly for best results
  • Suitable for: All skin types
  • Results: Immediate glow, long-term improvement over 4-6 weeks

Post-Treatment Care

After your Cosme Dermat Treatment, follow these guidelines for optimal results:

Avoid sun exposure for 24 hours; use SPF 50+ sunscreen daily

Use gentle, fragrance-free skincare products

Stay hydrated and drink plenty of water

Avoid makeup for at least 6 hours
